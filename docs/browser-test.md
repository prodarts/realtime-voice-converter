## iPhone Safari Checks

- Open the HTTPS GitHub Pages URL.
- Start recognition after granting microphone permission.
- Speak for at least 30 seconds.
- Stop, restart, and confirm new text appears.
- Copy cleaned text and paste it into Notes.
- Rotate the screen.
- Switch tabs and return.
- Confirm unsupported or interrupted states are understandable.
